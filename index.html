Low-Cost Solar-Powered Evaporative Cooling Storage System

Introduction & Problem

Food security and post-harvest losses are critical challenges, especially in developing regions where access to reliable electricity and refrigeration is limited or costly. According to the Food and Agriculture Organization (FAO), approximately 30% of perishable agricultural produce is lost due to inadequate storage facilities.

Conventional refrigeration systems are expensive, energy-intensive, and often inaccessible to small-scale farmers and rural communities. This project proposes the development of a low-cost, solar-powered evaporative cooling storage system to address these challenges.

Our Solution

Our system leverages the principles of evaporative cooling, combined with solar energy, to provide an affordable and sustainable solution for preserving perishable goods such as fruits, vegetables, and spices. It's designed to be reliable, environmentally friendly, and accessible to those who need it most.
